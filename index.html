The History Of Momoiro


Momoiro is a new venture from PT. Mimosa Tarte Indonesia, which previously brought Cheesess Cheese Cake to Indonesia. Located in Kota Kasablanka. Rosita Ngadiman, owner of PT. Mimosa Tarte Indonesia says this special pillow cake was born from an extensive product development process. Momoiro in Japanese is the color pink. The resulting cake It has a softer texture because it uses a special roast technique and imported quality ingredients.

Momoiro presents a chewy texture that has become the hallmark of this cake, Momoiro pillow cake is different from cakes others because the concoction of the recipe is adapted to the tastes of the Indonesian people's tongue. Every bite of the Momoiro cake prove its uniqueness; freshness, consistent chewy texture, the right combination of sweetness, cake filling and low degree of change in taste. As a result, Momoiro cake can be enjoyed without feeling uncomfortable. Momoiro doesn't use chemicals and preservatives, and implement strict quality control to ensure the quality of the pillow cake.
